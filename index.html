Challenges and Innovative Solutions

Traditional CCTV and Surveillance Systems

High cost of installation and maintenance

Traditional CCTV systems can be costly to install and maintain, as they require specialized equipment and the services of trained professionals.

Limited coverage and resolution

The coverage and resolution of traditional CCTV systems can be limited, making it difficult to capture clear and detailed footage of potential criminal activity.

Vulnerability to tampering and vandalism

Traditional CCTV systems can be vulnerable to tampering and vandalism, as the cameras and other equipment can be easily damaged or destroyed.

Dependence on human monitoring

Traditional CCTV systems often rely on human monitoring, which can be prone to errors and inefficiencies. This can lead to important footage being missed or overlooked, and can also put a strain on the staff responsible for monitoring the footage.

How does AI work in Video Surveillance Systems?

AI video surveillance systems can analyze and interpret footage in real-time, alerting security personnel to potential threats or suspicious activity. They can also learn over time to recognize patterns and improve accuracy. Traditional CCTV systems simply record footage, requiring human monitoring to identify any issues. Additionally, AI video surveillance can integrate with other systems such as access control and license plate recognition, increasing the overall effectiveness of security measures.
