Third Eye

Third Eye Video Analytics is state-of-the-art technology that leverages raw video footages and integrate them using sophisticated AI powered algorithms to bring Visibility and Insights to customers right on time AND orchestrate the associated security related tasks. Third Eye incorporates Deep Learning technology to 'learn', 'analyse' , and 'detect' the same information in new datasets which ensures correlation of events for the identified objects.
